Welcome to the Future of Handwritten Digit Recognition!

Handwritten Digit Recognition Web App

This app allows you to draw a digit on the canvas, and it will instantly recognize it using a deep learning model. The app uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on the MNIST dataset for highly accurate predictions.

How to Use the Handwritten Digit Recognition App

Step 1: Draw a Digit

Click and draw any digit from 0 to 9 on the canvas. The app will detect your drawing in real time.

Step 2: Click "Predict"

Once you've finished drawing the digit, click on the "Predict" button to get the prediction.

Step 3: View the Prediction

After a few seconds, the app will display the predicted digit and the confidence level of the prediction.
